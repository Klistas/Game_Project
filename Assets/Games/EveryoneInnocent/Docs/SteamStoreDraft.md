# Everyone Innocent Steam Store Draft

Status: internal draft only. Do not publish before the external gate passes.

## Working Title

Everyone Innocent

## One-Line Pitch

Clean up the crime scene together, then quietly make the evidence point at someone else.

## Short Description Draft

Everyone Innocent is a chaotic 2-4 player cleanup-and-blame party game where the team restores a ruined room before witnesses arrive, while each player secretly nudges the replay toward a different suspect.

## Long Description Draft

The museum is a mess, the evidence is everywhere, and the clock is not on your side.

In Everyone Innocent, players cooperate to make a disastrous room look normal again. Wipe spills, fix broken displays, hide suspicious objects, and keep the witness alarm low. But cooperation is only half the joke: the final CCTV-style trial replays what happened and decides who looks guilty.

Move a shard into someone else's bag. Rotate a camera at exactly the wrong moment. Swap a name tag. The team can survive together, but the replay rarely stays fair.

## Feature Bullets

- Restore the room before witnesses arrive.
- Secretly plant, move, or redirect visible evidence.
- Watch the final replay turn teamwork into accusations.
- Retry rooms with different blame routes.
- Built for short, loud sessions with friends.
